How does Scriptoman work?

the main procedures performed by Scriptoman

Upload your video/audio files and/or text files to Scriptoman.

Choose the language of the uploaded files and proceed to the next step

Auto Transcription

Scriptoman will convert your audio to text using AI.

Edit to Perfection.

Finally, once your automatic procedures are completed, you you can edit!

                                                          Features

Transcription

.   Processes video or audio recordings in the widest range of formats
.   Transcripts are delivered in .txt, .doc, .pdf, .json, .srt format (subtitles)
.   High-performance transcription editor.
.    Built-in player
.   Supports large files

Translation

.   Fast and high-quality processing of both audio/video and text files
.   Direct translation of transcribed files with Scriptoman
.   Supports as many as 110 languages
.   High-performance editor to improve results

Increase confidence

.   Indicates the degree of words that have been transcribed with a high level of confidence
.   Marks inaccurate words
.   Passages are marked chronologically
.   Highlights rare words with a below-average quality score

Ensuring the quality of editing

.   High-performance editor to improve results
.   Allows addition of notes and comments on the translation produced
.   Saving as .txt, .doc, .pdf, .srt (subtitling)
.   Foreseeable costs
.   Previsualization of the number of minutes/prepaid credit available

Save money

.   Low cost: Scriptoman costs a fraction of the cost of a human operator.
.   Quality delivery: The technology employed by RolaxIT helps reach a score of over 90% using lists of keywords, acronyms, etc.
.   Fast search for information: Audio files converted into text format allow quick searches for information, just as Google works.

Save the time

.   Saves a lot of time and boosts efficiency.
.    The experience in practice has shown that each hour of recording requires up to 4-5 hours of effective work to have it converted accurately into text format. With Scriptoman, the process time is cut down to half of the recording time.
